Introduction

Hi, my name is Robert. I started my adventure riding in 2016 with some small feats here in Switzerland. Short after i had a taste of the Adventure traveling and sightseeing, i fall love with it! I started to plan longer routes in the near countries to travel its famous roads, meet up with people and see they unique attractions. I have visited many countries till now, but i would like to travel all around in the world. You can follow my travels here on this blog.
